# notes

im very drawn to benjamins depiction of memory in the form of the text. the wistfullness of the tone is maybe the main characteristic marking it as reminisces. im reminded of Ivor Cutlor's Life in a Scotch Sitting Room... playful reembodiments of the state of mind of a childhood self... recalling the inexaustible wonder of it---placing the newborn ego subsequent to phenomena... 

i think of my own experience in unintentional age regression therapy on fucked up weed.... what stuck me was the uncertainty, the need to rescind mastery of the world to the expertise of others. Anxiety. But fascination. Wonder. Shadow Puppet shows on my bedroom wall.
