# notes

i feel sort of uncomfortable with the degree to which ive become interested in nazi/fascist related media.... obviously ive considered myself an antifascist for years now, but still, ive been very drawn to media which contain reference to it... illustration, this novel. clearly im not the only one, this is a pretty popular novel. the primary concern i have is the expression of my general interest in trangressive formations of things being an draw to fascist aesthetics with an unconscious development of a latent admiration for these things. at least in the realm of my conscious principles, any admiration for fascism is nauseating to me---the paranoia comes with the neurotic occupation my brain has with offense and wrongdoing.... simultaniously, a certain unbothered jaded tendency has attached itself to me.. so that in general im noticing degrees of  openness to evil ..... this novel mad me nauseous for a bit...  

im quite interested in the conditions qualifying these fabulous authors for inclusion in the glossary.... for many the association is obvious, but for others it isnt quite so... in some ways, keeping the association unacknowledged beyond their inclusion in the work makes their biographies more . i was thinking alot about the extent to which i engage with the lives of the writers i read. i go through so much material by so many people and almost never research their lived associations and occupations.. some of the novels by these authors where intriguing to me. of the ones with descriptions without allusions to nazi imagery, would i have read and cherished them, ignorant to the underlying affinities? if i had, to what extent would i accept being disturbed by my subliminal association?
